Future Wealth Starter Program

Free School Financial Literacy + Parent Consent-Based Financial Onboarding System

What This Program Does

We provide structured financial literacy education in primary schools and offer parents access to optional financial services through licensed financial institutions. All participation is voluntary and consent-based.

How It Works

  1. Schools receive free financial literacy program
  2. Learners complete workbook activities
  3. Parents attend optional workshops
  4. Consent-based interest registration
  5. Leads sent to licensed financial partners

Financial Partners

We collaborate only with licensed institutions including Capitec Bank, FNB, Standard Bank Group, and EasyEquities for optional financial onboarding.

Legal Disclaimer

The Future Wealth Starter Program is an educational initiative only. It does not provide financial advice, does not handle money, and does not open financial accounts. Any financial products are offered directly by licensed financial service providers. Participation is voluntary and subject to parental consent in accordance with POPIA (Protection of Personal Information Act).
