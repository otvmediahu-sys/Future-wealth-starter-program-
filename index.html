Future Wealth Starter Program

Financial Literacy for Children + Life Skills for Families

What This Program Does

The Future Wealth Starter Program is a school-based financial literacy initiative designed to help children understand money, saving, responsibility, and future planning in a simple, age-appropriate way.

Benefits for Children

  • Builds early money understanding
  • Teaches saving habits and discipline
  • Improves decision-making skills
  • Encourages goal setting from a young age
  • Develops real-life financial awareness

How the Program Works

  1. Schools receive free financial literacy materials
  2. Learners participate in fun classroom activities
  3. Workbooks are taken home to involve parents
  4. Optional parent engagement sessions are offered
  5. Families learn practical money skills together

Important Notice

The Future Wealth Starter Program is an educational initiative only. It does not provide financial advice, does not sell financial products, and does not handle money. All content is for learning purposes only. Parent participation is voluntary and consent-based in accordance with POPIA.
